**Cylindrical tubes cannot have square cross-sections.**
- Package is a cylindrical tube with circular cross-section
- FedEx claims 5" × 5" (implies square/rectangular base)
- Geometrically impossible for a cylinder
- Irrefutable physical fact

### Argument 2: Manufacturer Authority ⭐⭐⭐
**Yazoo certification is authoritative.**
- Official manufacturer specification: 9" × 4" × 4"
- Standardized commercial product
- Manufacturer specs override carrier measurements
- Valid certification (2025-2030)

### Argument 3: Unit Conversion Error ⭐⭐⭐
**FedEx's measurements contain mathematical errors.**
- 4 inches = 10.16 cm (correct)
- FedEx recorded: 12.70 cm (equals 5 inches)
- Mathematical proof of measurement error
- Either measured wrong or converted wrong

### Argument 4: Visual Evidence ⭐⭐⭐

- Delivery photo shows cylindrical tube
- No flat surfaces or 90-degree corners visible
- Visual estimates: ~9" × 4" (matches certification)
- Carrier's own documentation contradicts billing

### Argument 5: Third-Party Verification ⭐⭐

- ShipStation: 9" × 4" × 4"
- Pitman Creek Distribution: 9" × 4" × 4"
- All sources corroborate manufacturer specs
